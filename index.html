In the area of community is a beautiful nature and many tourists will be glad to visit such places and eat in our bakery.

Bakery SWOT analysis

The strengths of the project

Help of Community; Product quality; Assortment; Packaging; Location; The ability to change the range of products to respond flexibly to demand.

Vulnerable aspects of the project

Single point, Unrecognizable brand; No wholesale discount from suppliers due to low production volumes; The lack of established channels of delivery.

Opportunities and prospects

Create a distribution to Ski Resorts, close hotels, stores, fairs. The size of the room allows in the long term to increase the volume of production, to purchase additional equipment, as well as to introduce a hall for tasting, coffee and fresh beverages room.

Threats of the external environment

Lack of necessary knowledge on doing business in Canada; Increased prices for raw materials and primary products.
